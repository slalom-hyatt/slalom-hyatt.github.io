Hyatt launches iOS & Android App delivered by Slalom

The app enriches guest experience from booking to check-out and creates a platform for Hyatt to connect with their customers.

Going beyond the ordinary

At the beginning of our engagement in 2014, the extent of Hyatt’s mobile journey included simply booking a room. Hyatt lagged the hospitality industry, engaging Slalom in the midst of an onslaught of digital apps and experiences being offered by their competitors (Hilton, Starwood, Marriott and more). In the face of stiff competition and guests clamoring for something more, Hyatt and Slalom targeted a pilot mobile app to transform the stay experience. 

The app would provide meaningful features for Hyatt’s guests by giving them control. It aimed to delight users, provide contextually relevant information and services, and offer features beyond what was available elsewhere.

Capabilities for guests to manage their entire experience, from start to finish

Hyatt and Slalom pushed the boundaries in the pilot from an experience and feature perspective by providing car valet request, luggage assistance, uber integration, and even a message welcoming guests upon their arrival to the airport. Through the course of the project Slalom delivered the technology to enable Hyatt’s vision including full integration into their enterprise booking, reservation management, and service management systems while supporting and training a Hyatt development team for the future.

Take ownership. Get it done!

Over the last year, the joint team channeled feedback and learnings from the pilot app as well as delivery of major themes from the roadmap to achieve the “Hyatt Hotels” iteration launched August 2016. The result is an entirely new experience for guests, but also a new platform for Hyatt. It is a platform on which to gain further insight about guests and a direct channel. Through the course of the project, Slalom delivered to connect a growing base of hundreds of thousands of users to Hyatt’s brand and services!
